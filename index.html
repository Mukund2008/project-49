Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and the current captain of the India national team.Kohli was born on 5 nov'1988.Kohli is regarded as one of the best contemporary batsmen in the world. He plays for Royal Challengers Bangalore in the Indian Premier League, and has been the team's captain since 2013.Kohli has the best ever Test rating (937 points), ODI rating (911 points) and T20I rating (897 points).Kohli captained India Under-19s to victory at the 2008 Under-19 World Cup in Malaysia. After a few months later, he made his ODI debut for India against Sri Lanka at the age of 19. Initially having played as a reserve batsman in the Indian team, he soon established himself as a regular in the ODI middle-order and was part of the squad that won the 2011 World Cup.
